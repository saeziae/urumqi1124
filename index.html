24 November 2022, a fire broke out in a building in Xinjiang, China. Ten people were killed according to authorities. People raised questions that China's zero-COVID policy made the residents unable escape the building and interfered with the efforts of firefighters. The fire was a trigger of protests in several cities across China over the following days.
